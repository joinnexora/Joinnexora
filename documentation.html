1. Why We Established Nexora

Nigeria has over 220 million people. A growing proportion are educated, internet-connected, and eager to build careers online — yet they have almost no access to the global digital economy. At the same time, businesses worldwide need exactly the kind of human intelligence that Nigerians can provide.

The Gap: Global businesses need human input, testing, engagement, and data processing — but struggle to find affordable, reliable, scalable talent. Nigerian workers need access to global work — but have no trusted gateway.

Nexora was built to close this gap permanently. Not as a get-rich-quick scheme. Not as a speculative venture. As a legitimate, infrastructure-backed marketplace that treats workers as professionals and businesses as valued clients.

We created Nexora to:

Provide legitimate online earning opportunities
Real tasks from real companies, with pre-funded escrow. Not promises.
Reduce unemployment and underemployment
Give skilled, motivated Nigerians access to global income without leaving the country.
Enable earnings in foreign currency (USD)
Users earn, hold, and withdraw in USD — a stable store of value in a volatile economy.
Build a trusted ecosystem free from scams
Everything is transparent, verifiable, and independently auditable. No hidden conditions.

2. How Nexora Works

Nexora operates as a digital task and micro-work marketplace. Businesses post work. Workers complete it. The platform handles trust, quality control, and payments.

1
Businesses post tasks
Companies submit jobs with clear instructions, timeframes, and budget. Tasks are immediately placed in our verification queue.
SurveysApp TestingData LabelingSocial Engagement
2
Nexora reviews & approves tasks
Our compliance team verifies task legitimacy, confirms payment is secured in escrow, and checks instruction clarity before any worker sees the task.
3
Users browse and complete tasks
Registered, verified workers see available tasks matched to their skills. They complete work and submit proof through the platform.
4
Tasks are verified for quality
Our automated and manual review system checks submissions for accuracy and fraud. Legitimate completions are approved, typically within 24 hours.
5
Users get paid in USD
Approved earnings are instantly credited to the user's USD wallet. Funds can be withdrawn at any time via mobile money, bank transfer, or crypto.

3. How People Earn

Nexora provides four distinct income streams. Most users start with task completion and expand from there as they build reputation and experience on the platform.

Task Completion
Primary income stream. Complete surveys, test apps, label data, and engage with content. Pay: $0.50 – $5.00 per task.
Referral Program
Invite others using your unique referral link. Earn a percentage commission from their task activity — passively and indefinitely.
Affiliate Offers
Sign up for vetted partner platforms through Nexora. Earn commissions per action completed — ranging from $1 to $20 per conversion.
Performance Bonuses
High-quality, consistent workers receive bonus multipliers, priority access to premium tasks, and monthly performance rewards.
No investment required. Nexora earns from businesses, not from users. You never need to pay to access tasks or withdraw earnings. Your earnings grow based on consistency and quality — nothing else.

4. Task Types Explained

All tasks on Nexora are submitted by verified business clients and pre-funded before workers can accept them. Below are the main categories currently available:

Surveys — $0.50–$3.00
Share opinions on products, services, and consumer behaviour for global research firms. Typically 5–15 minutes per survey.
App Testing — $1.00–$5.00
Test real mobile and web applications for UX issues, bugs, and usability feedback. Requires a smartphone or computer.
Data Labeling — $0.80–$4.00
Label images, audio clips, and text to train AI models. One of the fastest-growing task categories globally.
Social Engagement — $0.50–$2.00
Engage with brand content, write verified reviews, or participate in moderated social campaigns. No fake activity permitted.

Bank Transfer (1–2 hrs)

Cryptocurrency (Instant)
Withdraw in USDC or USDT to any compatible crypto wallet address.
Minimum Withdrawal
Minimum withdrawal threshold: $5.00. No maximum limit on withdrawals.

7. Trust & Safety Framework

Trust is the most important part of this business. Here is precisely how we earn and maintain it:

Transparency — No Hidden Conditions
Every earning structure, fee, and condition is published publicly. If it isn't written down, it doesn't apply.
Verified Tasks Only
Every task is reviewed by our compliance team and must pass legitimacy, clarity, and payment-security checks before going live. Users never see unvetted tasks.
Escrow-Protected Payments
Client funds are locked in escrow before a task is assigned. Workers are guaranteed payment for approved work — the money physically exists before the task begins.
Anti-Fraud Systems
Automated and manual systems detect bot submissions, duplicate accounts, and coordinated fraud patterns. Verified genuine workers are protected and prioritised.
Professional, Honest Branding
Unlike scam platforms, Nexora publishes real payout ranges, avoids hype language, and treats users as workers — not as a source of deposits to extract.

8. Revenue Model

Nexora does not generate revenue from users. We earn exclusively from businesses and partners. This is a fundamental design choice that ensures our incentives always align with workers.

Business Task Fees
Businesses pay a platform fee on each task posted. Workers receive 100% of the advertised payout.
Affiliate Commissions
We earn partner commissions when users convert on affiliate offers distributed through the platform.
Business Subscriptions
Enterprise clients access advanced analytics, priority matching, and dedicated support via monthly plans.
Important: We never require users to pay a registration fee, task access fee, or withdrawal fee. If any platform claiming to be Nexora asks you to pay to earn or withdraw, it is fraudulent. Contact support immediately.

11. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nexora free to join?
Yes. Creating a Nexora account is completely free. You are never asked to pay to access tasks, receive earnings, or withdraw your balance.
How long does task approval take?
Most task submissions are reviewed and approved within 24 hours. Some complex tasks (like detailed UX reports) may take up to 48 hours. You will be notified by email when your submission is reviewed.
What happens if my task is rejected?
If a submission doesn't meet quality standards, you'll receive specific feedback explaining why. In most cases, you can revise and resubmit at no penalty — unless fraud or deliberate misuse is detected.
Do I need any special skills?
No special skills are required for most tasks. If you can follow instructions carefully, read English, and use a smartphone or computer, you can earn on Nexora. Advanced tasks (like data annotation or app testing) pay more and require slightly more attention to detail.
Is there a minimum age requirement?
Yes. You must be at least 18 years old to register and earn on Nexora. This is required for payment compliance and to protect minors.
How do I report a suspicious task?
Use the "Report Task" button on any task listing page, or email trust@joinnexora.online We take all reports seriously and investigate within 12 hours.
