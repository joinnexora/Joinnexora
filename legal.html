Privacy Policy

Nexora Technologies Ltd.
Effective: June 1, 2025
Version 1.0

Summary: We collect only what we need to operate the platform. We never sell your personal data. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

1. Who We Are

Nexora Technologies Ltd. ("Nexora," "we," "our," or "us") operates a digital task and micro-work marketplace at joinnexora.online and related mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

For questions about this policy, contact our Data Protection Officer at: privacy@joinnexora.online

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: email address, full name, date of birth, country of residence
  • Profile information: profile photo, skills, preferred task categories
  • Payment information: bank account details, mobile money numbers, crypto wallet addresses (stored encrypted)
  • Identity verification: government-issued ID documents (processed by our KYC partner, not stored directly by Nexora)
  • Task submissions: content, screenshots, or data you submit as part of completing tasks
  • Communications: messages sent to our support team

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage data: pages visited, tasks viewed and completed, time spent on platform
  • Cookies and similar technologies: see our Cookie Policy for details

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Nexora account
  • To match you with appropriate tasks based on your skills and location
  • To process and pay your earnings in USD
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • To communicate platform updates, new task types, and payment confirmations
  • To improve platform quality, task accuracy, and user experience
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do not: sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data to train AI models without explicit consent.

4. How We Share Your Information

  • Business clients: Task metadata only (e.g., task completion rates). We never share your personal identity with clients without your consent.
  • Payment processors: Payment details shared with regulated payment partners to execute withdrawals
  • KYC providers: Identity documents shared with our verification partner for compliance
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data when required by Nigerian law, court order, or regulatory authority
  • Service providers: Infrastructure, email, and analytics providers under strict data processing agreements

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active, plus 5 years thereafter for financial compliance purposes. Task submission data is retained for 2 years. You may request earlier deletion by contacting privacy@joinnexora.online — subject to legal retention obligations.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information in your account settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing your data for direct marketing purposes

To exercise any right, email privacy@joinnexora.online. We respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We use 256-bit AES encryption for stored sensitive data, TLS 1.3 for all data in transit, and strict access controls internally. We conduct regular security audits. No system is 100% secure — if you suspect unauthorised access to your account, contact us immediately.

8. Children's Privacy

Nexora is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us and we will remove the account promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email and a notice on the platform at least 14 days before taking effect. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.

Questions About Your Privacy?

Our Data Protection Officer is available to help.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember information about your visit — such as your login session, preferences, and usage behaviour — to improve your experience.

  • Supabase: Authentication sessions. Essential for login functionality.

You can control cookies through:

  • Email request: Contact privacy@joinnexora.online to request deletion of all analytics data associated with your account.

5. Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals. When DNT is enabled, we disable all optional analytics cookies automatically.

6. Updates to This Policy

We will update this policy if we add or change cookie usage. Changes will be communicated via the platform. Continued use after changes implies acceptance.

Refund Policy

Nexora Technologies Ltd.
Effective: June 1, 2025

Summary: Workers are always paid for approved work. Businesses may request refunds for unstarted or undelivered tasks. We handle disputes fairly and transparently.

1. For Workers (Earning Users)

Nexora does not charge workers any fees, so there is nothing to refund. Your concerns as a worker are:

Non-payment for completed work

If your task submission is approved and the earnings are not credited to your wallet within 48 hours, contact support@joinnexora.online with your task ID. We will investigate and resolve within 5 business days.

Disputed rejection

If you believe your submission was incorrectly rejected, you have 7 days from the rejection notice to raise a dispute. Submit a detailed appeal to support@joinnexora.online. Our review team will re-evaluate within 5 business days. If the appeal is upheld, your earnings will be credited within 24 hours.

Worker protection guarantee: If a task is cancelled by the client after you have begun work, you are entitled to a pro-rated payment for the verified portion completed, provided you have completed at least 20% of the task.

2. For Business Clients

Businesses pre-fund tasks through our escrow system. The following refund conditions apply:

SituationRefund EligibilityTimeframe
Task posted but not yet assigned to any workerFull refund of task fund + platform feeWithin 5 business days
Task partially completed (some workers started)Partial refund of unassigned portionWithin 7 business days
Task fully completed and approvedNo refund (work delivered)—
Task with systemic quality failure (>50% rejection rate)Full or partial refund at Nexora's discretionWithin 10 business days
Platform subscription feesPro-rated refund if cancelled within 7 days of billingWithin 7 business days

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email billing@joinnexora.online with:

  • Your account email address
  • The task ID or invoice number
  • The reason for your refund request
  • Any supporting evidence (screenshots, task descriptions)

We acknowledge all refund requests within 2 business days and aim to resolve within 10 business days.

4. Fraudulent Earnings

If an investigation confirms that a worker's earnings were obtained fraudulently (via bots, multiple accounts, or fabricated submissions), those earnings are forfeited. In serious cases, Nexora may pursue recovery through legal channels. Businesses are notified if fraudulent completions affect their tasks and compensated appropriately.

5. Currency & Processing

All refunds are issued in USD to the original payment source. Currency conversion losses resulting from exchange rate changes between payment and refund date are the responsibility of the requesting party. Bank or processing fees applied by third-party payment providers are non-refundable.

6. Escalation

If you are unsatisfied with the outcome of a refund decision, you may escalate to our Head of Operations at disputes@joinnexora.online. Escalated disputes are reviewed by a senior team member within 7 business days. Nexora's decision following escalation is final.
